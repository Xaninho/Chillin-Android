About the Project

An enjoyable lo-Fi streaming platform. Tons of new features to come!

The pursuit and the attraction of simplicity

Chillin' is a simple lo-fi streaming app with little BS and a ton of love. By combining an unique integrated 24/7 radio station with different playlists for every mood, you got all you could ask for a minimalist and different music experience. Just as Lo-Fi should be.

  • No pay wall restrictions. Besides access to the open-source code, you'll get all the new features as they come out.
  • Independent artists deserve to have their work known and spread. On each music you can find their social media links, be sure to give them some love ðŸ’œ
  • Simple and attractive user interface to help you chill with the least noise as possible.

While being developed for Android, this project will eventually spread to various platforms, including iOS, a web version and connection to various devices such as wearables and smart TVs.

Product Features

Quality

Quality before quantity; Chillin' will live by this motto. Expect to dive into a smooth app and listen to carefully chosen and curated beats.

Simple

A wholesome experience and a simple user-interface. Forget having to create an account. Just download and enjoy the tunes!

Free

No pay wall to access the best features whatsoever. You'll get all the coming ones as they come out and access to the code if you enjoy hackin' stuff!

More to Come

Besides releasing on more platforms, the app will be updated continously with more and more features to give you a genuinly good lo-fi chill experience.




What you want in one simple place

No need to leave a windows open to listen to some of your favourite streaming beats. In this app, you got all in one simple mobile place.

Ahhhh remember the nostalgia for simpler times, when you didn't have to listen to music using a service owned by a multi-million global corporation and kinda prefered the smaller cute stuff the most? Well this is one of those cute funny stuff you prefered the most. Give it a spin and enjoy!




Playlists for each Mood and Vibe

Carefully curated playlists for every chilly mood; to work, to listen to while you sip a cuppa joe in the morning, or maybe to get lost in an oriental atmosphere.

All of the music is carefully chosen and with the proper authorization of all the artists. All of the artists info is stored in the app, such and social media and stuff. Github documentation will present all of the artists info there as well.


More Coffee being converted to Code

What this means is more wholesome features are coming out constantly!

Wanna to save a specific beat? You got it. Wanna set a sleep timer to catch better Zzz's? Roger that. Maybe even listening offline for those off-track travels?

These and many more features are on development and coming out as times passes. More playlists, more platforms supported such as wearables, TVs, & desktop, and perhaps more radio stations, who knows!
